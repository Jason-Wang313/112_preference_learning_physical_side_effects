# Literature Map

## Preference Learning

Preference-based RL and RLHF already learn reward models from comparisons. This paper cannot claim preference learning itself is new.

## Safety And Constraints

Constraint learning, safe RL, and shielded planning already reduce undesirable behaviors.

## Physical Side Effects

The defensible contribution is a physical side-effect taxonomy and local evidence that taxonomy-aware preference learning distinguishes successful trajectories with different physical costs.
